About NU SCI Magazine

In September 2023, I joined NU SCI Magazine as a Newsletter Writer, initiating a weekly email newsletter focused on promoting industry engagement through concise updates on scientific developments. Our publication stands out through our innovative approach of presenting science in an engaging and non-traditional manner, fostering curiosity among readers from all backgrounds.

Mission

Establish a reputable newsletter that is accessible and engaging to all Northeastern audiences ranging from non-STEM majors to aspiring scientists.

We believe science should be exciting and accessible to everyone, regardless of their background. Through creative storytelling and visual design, we transform complex scientific concepts into compelling narratives that inspire wonder and understanding.

Impact & Approach

What sets our newsletter apart is our commitment to making science accessible without dumbing it down. We respect our readers' intelligence while acknowledging that not everyone has a scientific background.

Our Approach

  • Clarity First: Complex concepts explained in clear, jargon-free language
  • Visual Storytelling: Infographics and images that enhance understanding
  • Relevance: Connecting scientific discoveries to everyday life
  • Engagement: Interactive elements and thought-provoking questions
  • Diversity: Covering a wide range of scientific disciplines

Weekly Process

Creating each newsletter is a collaborative effort that requires careful planning and execution:

  1. Research Phase: Scan recent scientific publications and news for compelling stories
  2. Editorial Meeting: Collaborate with team to select topics and assign sections
  3. Content Creation: Write articles, design graphics, and format layout
  4. Review Process: Fact-checking and editorial review for accuracy
  5. Design Finalization: Polish visual elements and ensure mobile responsiveness
  6. Distribution: Send to subscriber base and promote on social media
