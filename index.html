About Me

I'm a senior at Northeastern University pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Astrophysics. My academic journey is driven by a fascination with how engineering can push the boundaries of exploration and innovation.

Through co-ops at Lutron Electronics and LineSpect, I developed strong skills in engineering design, rapid prototyping, tolerancing, and 3D printing—tools I have applied directly to my research in astrophysics at Northeastern's Aramaki Lab. This combination of practical engineering experience and a deep curiosity for fundamental physics allows me to tackle complex problems with both precision and imagination.

Beyond engineering, I founded Boston Clothing Outreach and host the Art of Inquiry podcast, where I share insights at the intersection of science, engineering, and art.
