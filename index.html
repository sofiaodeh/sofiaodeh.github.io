About Me

I'm a senior at Northeastern University pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Astrophysics. My academic journey is driven by a fascination with how engineering can push the boundaries of exploration and innovation.

I am passionate about exploring the universe through the lens of a mechanical engineer, combining technical expertise with curiosity-driven inquiry. Through co-ops at Lutron Electronics and LineSpect, I developed strong skills in engineering design, rapid prototyping, tolerancing, and 3D printing—tools I have applied directly to my research in astrophysics at Northeastern's Aramaki Lab.

Beyond engineering, I founded Boston Clothing Outreach and host the Art of Inquiry podcast, where I share insights at the intersection of science, engineering, and art. Whether developing innovative solutions or connecting with the community, I'm driven by a commitment to making a tangible impact through engineering.
