Product Overview: HABForecastr

Predicting Harmful Algal Blooms

HABForecastr is a revolutionary submersible microscope that predicts harmful algal blooms (HABs) 7 days in advance — providing critical time to act, not react. Think of it as a weather forecast for marine ecosystems, enabling proactive management of water resources.

  • 7-Day Advanced Warning: More time to implement preventive measures
  • Cost Savings: Prevents millions in damages and cleanup costs
  • Environmental Protection: Enables early intervention to minimize ecological impact
  • 1/6th the Cost: More affordable than current detection systems

Optical System Development

Complete Redesign of Optical Backplate

  • Stray Light Reduction: Implemented shielding solutions to dramatically improve imaging quality
  • Mirror Mount Simplification: Redesigned mounts for faster and more repeatable optical alignment
  • System Size Optimization: Minimized tube diameter while maintaining full field of view (FOV)
  • Component Integration: Combined NVIDIA mount and backplane into a single 3D-printed component
  • Assembly Refinement: Reduced screw count and part complexity for streamlined manufacturing

Technical Development

HAB Camera System

  • Housing Design: Optimized to fit within 4" copper pipe while maintaining optical performance
  • Optical Path Modeling: Reduced tube size while preserving full field of view
  • Mounting Flexibility: Added compatibility for both NVIDIA and Odroid boards
  • Beam Splitter Enhancement: Enabled interchangeability with dichroic mirrors for improved adjustability
  • Laser Diode Mount: Designed with fine horizontal/vertical adjustment and integrated cooling
